About Us

Discover the story behind Happy Paws and the team dedicated to making tails wag and purrs louder.

Our Story

It all started in 2015 when our founder, Sarah, rescued a scruffy, energetic terrier mix named Barnaby. While searching for high-quality, natural food and safe toys for him, she realized how difficult it was to find a single, trustworthy local store that truly cared about animal well-being.

Driven by her deep love for animals, Sarah decided to create the place she was looking for. What began as a small corner shop providing premium kibble has blossomed into Happy Paws Pet Shop—a comprehensive community hub. Today, we offer not just top-tier products, but a place where pet parents can connect, learn, and give their furry companions the best life possible.

Our Mission

"To enrich the lives of pets and their people by providing exceptional care, premium nutrition, and endless love."

At Happy Paws, we believe every animal deserves respect, comfort, and joy. We carefully vet every brand we carry, ensuring that your pets are eating healthy and playing safe. Whether it's a dog, cat, bird, or small mammal, our love for animals drives everything we do.
